4 The Overview View

The Overview view allows the user to view and sort the human review queue and to select a record for review in the Review view. The view provides only summary information about the queued record. More detail on the records may be viewed in Review.

When initially launched, Overview brings up a screen, shown in , displaying the first 100 records from the human review queue in the order that they were entered in the queue. Clicking on the "Next" and "Previous" buttons allows for paging through the queue 100 records at a time. Clicking on the "Refresh" button removes any already-reviewed records from the queue. Clicking on a record and then clicking the "Review Selection" button or double-clicking a record will bring up the selected record in the Review view.

The queue can be sorted in any desired order by clicking on the appropriate column header. For instance, shows the human review queue sorted by last name. Figure 74 shows the queue sorted in reverse order of last name with a second click. Note that clicking on the column header sorts the entire queue, not just the 100 records currently displayed on the screen.
