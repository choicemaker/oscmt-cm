2 Support Vector Machines

The SVM plugins com.choicemaker.cm.ml.svm.base and com.choicemaker.cm.ml.svm.gui provide support for support vector machines (Nello Cristianini and John Shawe-Taylor 2000). They are based on libsvm (Chang 2002).

2.1 Introduction

The plugin supports both classification and regression. Support vector machines do not consider the decision of clues, e.g., match- and differ-clues are treated the same.

2.2 Strengths and weaknesses

Support vector machines have the following strengths:

  • Takes a composite view. Requires few composite clues.

Support vector machines have the following weaknesses:

  • Difficult to see how well a specific clue works.
  • Slow in training for certain parameter values.
