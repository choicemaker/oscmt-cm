Multi clues can also be used in non-boolean clue sets. Figure 42 gives an example of a numeric multi clue. The choice of -1 in case one or both of the names are invalid is arbitrary. It often makes sense to replace a boolean multi clues in a non-boolean clue sets by a single clue of numeric type.
